social mission

As a non-profit tea company, Taaling Tea supports local textile industries in Gambia through native entreprenuership.

Gambian Tea

Each box of Taaling Tea is paired with a proverb from my father Ansumana Kunjo's collection of Mandinka proverbs, as well as a hand-sketched image of everyday Gambian life on the cover. Taaling is more than just tea, it it a movement to foster connection between people and between people and what they consume
